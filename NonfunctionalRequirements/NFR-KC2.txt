REQ-ID: KC2
Description: The product waiting list shall display the exact number of tickets the customer needs
Type: Support Requirement
Rationale: To improve reallocation efficiency, staff should be able to see the number of tickets customers have ordered on the waiting list and for which events.
Fit Criterion: Waiting list must not only display the customer name/ID but also number of tickets ordered and for which event.  
Originator: Staff
